Women in Humanities, Preservation, and Emerging Media

About

Women in Humanities, Preservation and Emerging Media (WiHPEM) is a developing professional organization focused on providing inclusive safe spaces, support, and supplemental education for women working at the intersection of archiving, preservation, and emerging media fields.

WiHPEM’s goal is to provide opportunities for women to support other women as they progress professionally through activities based in collaboration, mentorship, and knowledge-sharing with the ultimate goal of encouraging meaningful contributions from women in our fields.

Our activities currently include collecting resources and organizing educational workshops, discussions and training sessions around skills and topics relevant to the preservation landscape. We are also exploring models for providing mentorship, feedback and professional guidance, both to our members and to young pre-professionals.
